FAQ

Straight answers
to common questions.

What is the $197 audit and why should I pay for it?
It's a real paid review of your online presence — Google Business, website, reviews, and your top 3 competitors. You'll see exactly what's costing you calls and what fixing it would mean. The $197 applies toward any package if you move forward, so it goes toward your investment either way.
What's the difference between Foundation and Authority?
Foundation gets you off a bad or outdated website. You'll look professional and legitimate. Authority is the full transformation — real photos from your location, review systems, missed-call recovery, and Google authority. Foundation is baseline. Authority is dominance.
How long does it take?
Foundation is delivered without agency delays — typically 5–7 business days from when we finalize your content. Authority takes slightly longer due to the content session, typically 10–14 days total. Need it faster? Rush delivery gets it done in 3 days.
Do I own the website when it's done?
Yes — 100%. You own the domain, the hosting account, and the site itself. No monthly platform fees, no lock-in. I hand everything over cleanly when we're done.
What is the missed-call text-back system?
When a customer calls and you don't answer — because you're on a job — they automatically receive a text within seconds: "Hey, this is [Your Business]. Sorry I missed you — how can I help?" Most of them respond. Without this system, most of them call your competitor instead.
How does the review generation system work?
After you complete a job, the system automatically sends the customer a text asking them to leave a Google review. Simple, automatic, and it works. More recent reviews mean higher Google rankings and more trust from new customers.
What does the monthly retainer actually include?
Google Business posts, new project photos added monthly, review system management, missed-call system monitoring, website edits, and a monthly snapshot showing your calls, profile views, and reviews gained. Two to three hours of work per month that keeps your online presence ahead of competitors.
Is $4,500 worth it for a contractor?
One roofing job is $8,000–$15,000. One HVAC installation is $5,000–$10,000. One remodel is $20,000+. If the Authority package brings in one additional job per month — which a proper website and Google presence consistently does — it pays for itself in the first week. The question isn't whether it's worth it. It's whether you can afford to keep losing jobs to competitors who just look better online.
