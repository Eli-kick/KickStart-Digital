FAQ

Straight answers
to common questions.

How long does it take?
7 business days from when we finalize your content and direction. Need it faster? Rush delivery gets it done in 3 days for an additional $250.
Do I own the website when it's done?
Yes — 100%. You own the domain, the hosting account, and the site itself. I hand everything over cleanly when we're done. No monthly fees, no lock-in.
What if I don't like how it looks?
Every package includes revisions. You'll see a draft before anything goes live and we refine it until you're happy. Not satisfied? I'll make it right or refund your deposit.
Do I need to provide photos or content?
For Launch and Growth, it helps to have some photos and a basic description of your services — but I can work with what you have. For Authority, we handle all the photos on the content day.
What's included in the content day for Authority?
We schedule a 2–4 hour session at your location. During that time we shoot everything your website needs — team headshots, location and storefront photos, work-in-progress shots, before/after project photos, and short branded video clips. Everything is delivered ready to use on your website.
What kinds of businesses do you work with?
Local service businesses — roofers, HVAC companies, painters, landscapers, remodelers, flooring companies, pressure washing, countertops, cabinets, and similar trades. If you do physical work for local customers, I'm built for you.
Do you do ongoing SEO or marketing?
I build websites with a solid, search-friendly structure so Google can find and index you properly. I don't offer ongoing SEO management or paid ads — I focus on building you the best website possible.
Is the $750 really worth it?
For most service businesses, one solid job can more than cover the cost of the website. The goal is to give customers a better first impression and make it easier for them to contact you — that pays off over time.
