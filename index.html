What exactly is the $197 Contractor Audit?
A real, paid review of your online presence — website, Google Business profile, reviews, and your top 3 competitors. You'll see exactly what homeowners see before they call, what's costing you jobs, and what it would take to fix it. The $197 applies toward any package if you move forward.
What's the real difference between Foundation and Authority?
Foundation stops the embarrassment — you get a clean, professional website and a basic Google presence. Authority makes you the obvious choice — real photos from your location, an optimized Google profile, automatic review generation, missed-call recovery, and a system that keeps leads from going cold. Foundation is the floor. Authority is the competitive advantage.
How does the missed-call recovery system work?
When a homeowner calls you and you don't answer — because you're on a roof or a job site — they automatically receive a text within 60 seconds: "Hey, this is [Your Company]. Sorry I missed your call — how can I help?" Most of them respond. Without this system, most of them call your competitor instead.
How does the review generation system work?
After you complete a job, the system automatically sends the homeowner a text asking for a Google review. Simple, automatic, and consistent. More recent reviews mean better Google visibility and more trust from new homeowners searching for contractors.
How long does it take to build?
Foundation is delivered without agency delays — typically 5–7 business days. Authority takes slightly longer due to the content session, typically 10–14 days total. Need it faster? Rush delivery gets it done in 3 days.
Do I own the website when it's done?
Yes — 100%. You own the domain, the hosting account, and everything we build. No monthly platform fees, no lock-in. I hand everything over cleanly when we're done. It's yours.
Why is Growth Support required after Authority?
Because the systems installed during Authority — missed-call recovery, review generation, Google visibility management, and homeowner follow-up — are active systems that run every month. They require ongoing monitoring, management, and optimization to keep working properly. Growth Support is not a marketing retainer. It's the operational management of the infrastructure we built for your business. Without it, the systems go unmonitored and leads start falling through the cracks.
How does Authority pay for itself?
One roofing job is $8,000–$15,000. One HVAC installation is $5,000–$10,000. One larger remodeling project is $20,000+. When Authority makes a homeowner trust your company enough to call you instead of a competitor — and it will — the return comes back fast. This isn't a website cost. It's an investment in how your market perceives you, and that perception keeps paying you back on every job it wins you.
